Adventures and Experiences

Welcome to the Sherton Maldives Hotel's centre of excitement! Your gateway to wonderful experiences in paradise is our website on adventures and activities. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Maldives' blue oceans and pristine white beaches, immerse yourself in a world of exhilarating experiences and peaceful pursuits.

Explore the underwater delights with our licenced diving instructors, go snorkelling in coral gardens, or dive into the beautiful ocean to find abundant marine life.
Try your hand at water activities like kayaking, windsurfing, and jet skiing if you're looking for some thrill. If unwinding is what you're after, rest by the infinity pool or treat yourself to revitalising spa services. Every visitor will find something they enjoy from cultural island tours to romantic sunset cruises thanks to our wide variety of activities. Read More...

Leave It All Behind

Set amidst a lush tropical garden facing the majestic Indian Ocean, Shine Spa for Sheraton is a sanctuary of serenity. Our spa offers a wide range of holistic treatments that cater to your every need, from traditional aromatherapy and Ayurvedic massages to locally inspired deep ocean therapies. Immerse yourself in tranquility and let our skilled therapists take you on a journey to rejuvenate your body, soothe your mind, and awaken your spirit.

WATERSPORTS

Experience the thrill of watersports like never before. Whether it's jet skiing, paddleboarding, or windsurfing, our crystal-clear waters provide the perfect playground for aquatic adventures. Dive in and make a splash!

EXCURSIONS

Explore the beauty of our surroundings with our curated excursions. From guided island tours to sunset cruises, immerse yourself in the natural wonders of the Maldives. Adventure awaits!

SCUBA DIVING

Dive into the depths of the ocean and discover a world of wonder with our scuba diving experiences. Explore vibrant coral reefs, encounter exotic marine life, and create unforgettable underwater memories.

Family Adventures in Sheraton Maldives

Our kids' clubs offer children of ages 4-12 years a chance to go solo and join beachfront crab races, indoor educational activities, or snorkel under supervision.

Coral Propagation Project

Follow our experienced marine biologist and learn about the resort’s coral program in this fun and interactive coral-fragment plantation activity.

Diving

Did you know North Male Atoll is home to over 40 world-class diving points, all within close proximity to Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort & Spa.

Sunset Dolphin Cruise

Cruise along the Indian Ocean on a traditional Maldivian Cruise Dhoni, soak in the breathtaking sunset and get your camera ready for a moment with these magnificent creatures.
